# OctoAcme Project Management Docs

Welcome to the OctoAcme Project Management documentation hub. Here you will find comprehensive guides to all project management workflows, roles, and checkpoints used at OctoAcme for consistent, effective delivery.

## Project Management Processes (Summary)
- **Initiation**: Validation of business need, creation of a one-pager, stakeholder alignment, and a lightweight plan.
- **Planning**: Breaking work into increments, building a prioritized backlog with acceptance criteria, estimation, and risk/dependency management.
- **Execution & Tracking**: Using structured rhythms (standups, weekly syncs, demos), PR workflows, quality testing, reporting, and escalation paths for blockers.
- **Risk Management & Communication**: Maintaining a risk register, regular updates, status templates, and clearly documented escalation paths.
- **Release & Deployment**: Follows checklist-driven deployments, incident/rollback procedures, and clear release notes.
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**: Post-sprint or release reviews, learnings capture, and ongoing improvement tracking.
- **Roles and Personas**: Clear definitions of PM, PdM, developer, and key contributor responsibilities.
